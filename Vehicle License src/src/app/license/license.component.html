Learner's License

Need for a Driving License

No person shall drive a motor vehicle in any public place unless he holds an effective driving licence issued to him by the Licensing Authority, authorising him to drive the vehicle.

Age limit to obtain Driving Licence

  • An applicant who has completed eighteen years of age is eligible to apply for a driving licence to drive a motor cycle without gear subject to the condition that the parent or guardian should furnish a declaration in the manner prescribed.
  • An applicant who has completed twenty years of age will be eligible for applying for a licence to drive a transport vehicle or HeavyMotorVehicles.
